    Welcome to the official website of BREAD FROM HEAVEN OUTREACH INTERNATIONAL

    Registration status of the organisation

    The NGO is fully registered under the NGO Bureau which is a semi-autonomous body under the Ministry of Internal Affairs Uganda.

    Our achievement and commitment

    We are committed to serving the most vulnerable people in Uganda covering activities in these programs among which include care and protection, education, health etc. Our main focus as of now is mainly in the central part of Uganda most especially in MPIGI district but we have plans in future to reach to other areas of the country.

    Our mandate and way forward

    We achieve this through constant service improvement and boundless innovation. We have carefully developed this website to address all your information and procedural needs. We have opened up a number of communication platforms such as Online Chat, Contact forms and Telephone lines.

    Feel free to send us feedback through our various communication channels. We welcome positive criticism and feedback.

    Vote of thanks

    I want to end by thanking you for visiting our website and encourage you to visit again. Thank you.

    For God and my Country
